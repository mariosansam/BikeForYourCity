Presenting BIKE FOR YOUR CITY

Thanks to the innovative iBike developed by FabLab Limerick, you will give feedback of the amount of noise, pollution and problems you have to suffer while riding your bike.

Learn More

How it works?

Just 4 steps. And it's free!

Sign up!

This is the first step. Send your information and we we'll contact you.

Receive your iBike

Attach it to your bike. Download the Bike For Your City app and pair it to your smartphone via Bluetooth.

Use your bike

The iBike will automatically send information to your smartphone and will be later updated to our database.

Win rewards

We want to encourage people to use their bikes and make Limerick a better city. Let us help you

Have you seen something wrong?

It can be a dangerous spot in the street or a not well lit area. Let us know using the in-app map. Check your statistics and discover how you have helped others like you.

Feedback

We can know the amount of dB in the city and try to improve the isolation in certain places.

Noise

Each person going to the University/work by bike is one car less in the streets. Now we can know the pollution quantity all around the city.

Pollution

Basing on your GPS track we can check if the speed limit in a certain area is right or if it would be better to create other paths/change the speed limit/change the route.
